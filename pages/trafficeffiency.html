AI TECHNOLOGY

Automatic Illegal Parking Detection

Our project doesn't just look for empty slots; our custom smart cameras monitor active red zones and fire lanes automatically.

How the AI System Protects Roads:

1. Object Localization: Identifies stationary vehicles in restricted lanes instantly.

2. Time Tracking: Triggers an alert if a car stays over the 2-minute loading limit.

3. Automated Alerts: Sends a real-time notification directly to city council control rooms.
