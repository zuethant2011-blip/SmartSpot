Topic = Smart parking

DQ = How can year8 students create a digital platform for local residents to educate awareness about sustainable city while sharing solutions and ideas?

EQ = How can we create a simple website to raise awareness about how smart technology helps to reduce parking issues and improve traffic efficiency in urban areas?

Focus Area
Our project focuses on solving parking problems in busy urban areas using technology. We will create a website  for a smart parking system that shows how drivers can quickly find available parking spaces. Our website will include features like a parking map idea, instructions on how the system works, and how technology can reduce traffic and save time. Our goal is to demonstrate how a simple digital solution can improve parking efficiency in cities and make driving less stressful.
